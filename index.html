Who I am


My name is Hayden Wang, I'm a junior Web Designer & Front-end Developer from Taiwan.


My biggest passion is to design what you can see on the internet, I love design and develop web sites because I think that this is a powerful way to express myself and communicate/connect with people all over the world.


I've studied Engineering of mechanical in college(well... actually I am always coding), after graduate and working in laptop company as a software engineer for more than 2 years, I suddenly figure out that the most important thing I have to do is using my coding experience to develope and construct something to help more people improving their ability about what they can do on the internet.


Every day and night, I keep learning and discovering this whole new world full of HTML, CSS(including CSS3), javascript/jQuery, Mobile App UI, uh...maybe a little bit PHP/MySQL.
